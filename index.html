About

I am Chase Bank Endowed Professor and Assistant Professor of Finance in the College of Business at Louisiana Tech University. My work focuses on asset pricing, market microstructure, labor-related corporate policies, and technological change.

I am particularly interested in questions that connect rigorous finance research to practical issues faced by firms, investors, workers, and policymakers.
