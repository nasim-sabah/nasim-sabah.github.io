About

I am Chase Bank Endowed Professor and Assistant Professor of Finance in the College of Business at Louisiana Tech University.

My research focuses on financial markets, investor behavior, labor and corporate policy, and the economic effects of emerging technologies. I am particularly interested in questions that connect rigorous empirical finance research to practical issues faced by firms, investors, workers, and policymakers.
